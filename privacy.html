Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

Entry Log+ is designed to keep your health journal private. All information entered into the app is stored locally on your device.

Information collection

Entry Log+ does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information or health information.

The app does not use:

  • User accounts
  • Analytics
  • Advertising
  • Tracking technologies
  • Third-party data collection services
  • Cloud storage or cloud synchronization

Local storage

Health entries, notes, and settings remain on your device. Data leaves the app only when you choose to export or share a report or archive.

Data deletion

You can delete individual entries or clear all saved history from within the app. Removing the app from your device also removes its locally stored data, unless you previously exported an archive.
